About Zopper

    Zopper is a hyperlocal marketplace that helps consumers to find products and check offline prices. 
    The Company connects buyers with electronic stores in their neighborhood. Using the Zopper's app, 
    users can discover, compare and purchase various electronic products from their homes and offices, 
    and get the product from their nearby stores.
